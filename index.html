About Me

Passionate about visual storytelling and illustration!

I’m a Graphic Designer and Motion & Animation Designer currently studying at CodeToInspire. My work focuses on illustration, branding, poster design, and visual storytelling.

I enjoy turning ideas into meaningful visuals and exploring how motion and animation can bring designs to life.

I work mainly with Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, and I’m currently learning After Effects to expand my motion design skills. I’m eager to be part of creative projects, collaborate with teams, and grow as a professional designer.
